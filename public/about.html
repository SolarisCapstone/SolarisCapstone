About This Platform

The Graduation Planner helps UNC Charlotte students organize their academic journey by providing personalized tools to plan, track, and achieve graduation goals.

Graduation Planner

Design your ideal path to graduation, semester by semester, with prerequisite tracking and personalized scheduling.

Course Catalog

Browse available courses, view prerequisites, and discover electives to enhance your academic experience.

Dashboard

Quickly view your degree progress, completed credits, upcoming deadlines, and important academic milestones.

Degree View

Visualize your full degree requirements and see how completed courses fit into your overall graduation plan.
